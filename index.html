About Us

Journey of

Started by N.Ganapadiya Pillai in 1950 under the name "Aruna Appalam" and formally registered by the government in 1958, the business journey expanded throughout Tamil Nadu. After that Mr. G. Thirumurugan s/o N. Ganapathy Pillai, rebranded and refined it as "Madurai Pandian Appalam" and started its business journey with a new luster. Exports starting from Malaysia and expanded it to Singapore, Sri Lanka, Maldives, Saudi Arabia and Canada, United Kingdom, Quatar, Kenya. Then it expanded beyond Tamil Nadu to Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka and Andaman.

Madurai Pandian Appalam is also a top leading seller in various major retail hyper markets including Reliance, D Mart and Pothys. First class quality and on time delivery is our primary moto of the company
